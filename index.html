Welcome

Welcome to Sarah Brosnan Hair, a welcoming and creative space where great hair and good vibes come together.

With a passion for modern colour and timeless style, Sarah is dedicated to helping each client look and feel their best. Specialising in colouring, highlights, balayage, cutting, upstyling, and extensions, tailoring every service to suit your unique look, lifestyle, and personality.

Whether you're after a soft, sun-kissed balayage, a complete colour transformation, or a polished upstyle for a special occasion, we bring skill, creativity, and attention to detail to every appointment.
